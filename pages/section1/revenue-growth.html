1.2.1 Revenue & Growth

Summarize your recent revenue performance, headcount, and growth strategy. Narrative responses support rich formatting; staffing counts and fiscal-year fields are plain text or numbers.

1.2.1.1 Most Recent Fiscal Year Required

Identify the most recent fiscal year for which audited or reviewed financials are available.

1.2.1.2 Staffing Required

Report your total headcount and the subset of staff that are licensed clinicians.

1.2.1.3 3-Year Revenue Narrative Required

Walk us through your revenue trajectory over the past three fiscal years. Describe the drivers of growth, any non-recurring events, and the mix of revenue by service line. Formatting is supported.

1.2.1.4 Growth Strategy Required

Outline your growth plan for the next 2–3 years, including target markets, investments in clinical capacity, and any planned expansions of telehealth infrastructure. Formatting is supported.

2.2.2.2 Accreditation & Industry Standards

Document your primary behavioral-health accreditation and the broader quality management program behind it.

2.2.2.2.1 Primary Accreditation Required

Identify the accrediting body, the effective date, and your accreditation certificate or identifier.

2.2.2.2.2 Accreditation Narrative Required

Describe the scope of your accreditation, any additional certifications (CARF, COA, NCQA, URAC, HITRUST), and the continuous readiness practices you maintain between surveys. Formatting is supported.

2.2.2.2.3 Quality Management Program Required

Describe your quality management program — governance, performance improvement methodology, KPIs you monitor, and how clinical leadership reviews outcomes. Formatting is supported.

2.1.2 Care Coordination & Integrations

Describe how your platform coordinates care across the network and how it integrates with Meridian's existing EHR, HIE, and ancillary systems.

2.1.2.1 Primary EHR Integrations Required

List the EHR systems you integrate with today, using plain text (e.g., "Epic, Cerner/Oracle, NextGen, Welligent").

2.1.2.2 HIE Participation

Identify the Health Information Exchanges (HIEs) or regional networks you currently participate in.

2.1.2.3 Care Coordination Plan Required

Describe how your platform supports warm handoffs, referrals, and collaborative care between primary care providers, behavioral health clinicians, and care managers. Formatting is supported.

2.1.2.4 Integration Approach with Client Systems Required

Describe your technical integration approach — APIs, standards (FHIR R4, HL7 v2, CDA), scheduling sync, SSO, and directory federation. Formatting is supported.

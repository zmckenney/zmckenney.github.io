2.1.2 Integration Plan

Detail how the proposed solution will integrate with existing government systems and third-party services.

2.1.2.1 Integration Approach Required

Describe your overall approach to integrating with existing agency systems, including middleware, APIs, and data exchange protocols.

2.1.2.2 Target Systems Required

Select all existing agency systems your solution will need to integrate with.

2.1.2.3 API Specifications Required

Describe the APIs your solution will expose and the estimated number of endpoints.

2.1.2.4 Data Migration Strategy Required

Describe how existing data will be migrated to the new system, including volume estimates and validation approach.

2.1.2.5 Integration Testing Required

Describe your approach to integration testing, including environments, tools, and acceptance criteria.

2.1.1 Clinical Service Model

Describe the clinical service model your platform will enable for Meridian's network, the populations it addresses, and how outcomes are measured.

2.1.1.1 Service Model Summary Required

Give your proposed service model a short name and note the primary care populations it's designed to serve.

2.1.1.2 Clinical Approach & Methodology Required

Describe the clinical approach, modalities, and care pathways supported by your platform (e.g., CBT, DBT, measurement-based care, collaborative care). Formatting is supported.

2.1.1.3 Evidence Base Required

Summarize the peer-reviewed evidence, published studies, or outcomes data that support your service model. Formatting is supported.

2.1.1.4 Outcome Measurement Required

Describe how you measure clinical outcomes (instruments used, cadence, thresholds) and how data are reported back to clinicians and clients. Formatting is supported.

2.2.1 Privacy & Data Protection

Describe how your organization protects PHI across its lifecycle, and provide the personnel and dates that anchor your privacy program.

2.2.1.1 Privacy Officer Required

Provide the name and email of the individual responsible for your privacy program.

2.2.1.2 Last Privacy Audit

When was your most recent third-party privacy or security audit completed?

2.2.1.3 HIPAA Privacy Program Required

Describe your HIPAA privacy program: how you manage minimum necessary disclosures, patient rights workflows, authorizations, accounting of disclosures, and training. Formatting is supported.

2.2.1.4 Data Protection Practices Required

Describe your technical safeguards for PHI: encryption at rest and in transit, key management, access control, logging, and breach response. Formatting is supported.

2.3 Implementation Approach

Describe how you'll stand the platform up for Meridian — proposed dates, project leadership, phased milestones, risk mitigation, and training rollout.

2.3.1 Proposed Timeline Required

Provide the proposed kickoff date, go-live date, and the name of the project manager who would own the engagement.

2.3.2 Implementation Phases & Milestones Required

Describe your implementation phases, major milestones, and key deliverables. Use a numbered list to make the phasing easy to follow. Formatting is supported.

2.3.3 Risk & Mitigation Plan Required

Identify the top risks to a successful rollout (clinical adoption, integration complexity, data migration, compliance) and the mitigations you'll put in place. Formatting is supported.

2.3.4 Training & Adoption Strategy Required

Describe how you'll train clinicians, care managers, and administrative staff, and how you'll measure and drive sustained adoption post go-live. Formatting is supported.
